About Us

Strong Body App

A Mobile app (Android and iOS) for people who cares about their healthy lifestyle and cannot imagine their life without the gym and exercising. This app designed to help each user to set their GYM goals, choose optimal workout program, and find the best exercises for healthy lifestyle. The App provides video tutorials for exercises and includes healthy eating tips and diet programs carefully designed by the best nutritionists.

Moreover, a mobile app where a user can select a best fit program (CrossFit, weightlifting, cardio), set up major goals (lose weight, gain muscles weight, or just get active and healthy, etc.) or select their available equipment they have. Then app would provide an optimal exercise program for the user, show the list of exercises for different groups of muscles workout , suggesting different workout video to follow, diet plan and tips to make it customize as needed. In addition, help to find a local gym(s) nearby the user's location. Finally, the app will have different modes: beginner, advanced, professional
